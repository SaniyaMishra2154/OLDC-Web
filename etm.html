Educational Technology
Media (ETM) Lab

What is the ETM Lab?

The Educational Technology Media (ETM) Lab at OLDC supports learning through professionally produced audio and video educational content. It helps learners understand concepts better by offering flexible, multimedia-based learning resources alongside traditional study materials.

How
Students Benefit ?

The ETM Lab enhances the learning experience by providing learners with high-quality video and audio content that simplifies complex topics, supports self-paced learning, and makes education more accessible, engaging, and flexible.

Allowing learners to study at their own pace and convenience, thereby supporting flexible learning pathways and improved learner engagement.

Supporting different learning preferences, including visual and auditory learners

Providing video and audio content to simplify complex topics and support clearer conceptual understanding.

Improving accessibility for learners who benefit from audio-based resources, including those who prefer auditory learning methods.

WHAT THE LAB PRODUCES

Video lectures and academic demonstrations

Audiobooks and audio learning materials

Podcasts and interviews with educators and subject experts

Educational bulletins and instructional tutorials
