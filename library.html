Rules &

Regulations

Postgraduate Courses :

The library fee is Rs. 400/- per student per annum, with a refundable security fee of Rs. 500/-.

Library
Membership

Renewal of Membership

Students are required to renew their membership every year by presenting the fee receipt and the renewed ID card for the current academic session.

Membership to the library is available to students, faculty members and non-teaching members of SOL. To gain membership, individuals must mandatorily register and present the necessary documents for identification purposes.

Loan Privileges

Loan privileges in the SOL library are designed to cater to students across various academic programmes that are offered by SOL.

Postgraduate Courses: PG students are permitted to borrow four books for a period of 45 days.

Honours Courses: Students enrolled in Honours courses are entitled to borrow three books for a period of 30 days.

B.A.(Prog.)/B.Com.: Students enrolled in these courses can borrow two books for a period of 30 days.

General Library Guidelines

Personal Belongings

Bags, briefcases, personal items and books from other libraries aren’t allowed inside the SOL library. The library isn’t responsible for the loss or damage to personal belongings left unattended on the library premises. Items such as mobile phones, cash, laptops, tablets etc. cannot be deposited at the property counter and must be taken back by the student on the same day.

Library Hours

PCP Classes & Holidays

Library will remain open during PCP classes as per schedule and on other holidays, except for the National holidays and Gazetted holidays.

Operational Hours

The library is open from 9:00 AM to 5:30 PM on working days at all the study centres of SOL.

Library Resources and Services

Reference Section and Reading Hall

It is a vital resource hub for students and faculty members. It houses a diverse collection of encyclopedias, dictionaries, handbooks, research reports and other reference materials that can’t be borrowed but can be accessed within the library. The reading hall at the library is open for both students and faculty members, even on Sundays. SOL library provides access to a wide range of periodicals, magazines and newspapers to keep the students and faculty members updated with current affairs, research trends and academic insights. The collection includes national and international journals, subject specific magazines and leading dailies.
