PRAGYAN

Platform Of Remote Accessibility Towards Guiding Youth For Academic Nurturing

Student Login > How PRAGYAN Works > Support >

PRAGYAN is OLDC’s Learning Management System designed to support open and distance learners through flexible access to learning resources, online assessments, and academic progress tracking—all in one platform.

➜ Visit COL Radio

What Students Can Do on
PRAGYAN

PRAGYAN is not just a platform—it’s an evolving learning ecosystem. As academic needs, technologies, and learner expectations change, PRAGYAN continues to adapt to support effective, inclusive, and future-ready education.

01

Learn Without Limits

Access Self-Learning Materials (SLMs), academic resources, and course content anytime, anywhere—designed to support independent and flexible learning.

02

Take Assessments

Complete internal assessments digitally, eliminating physical submissions and making evaluation more accessible, transparent, and efficient.

03

Track Your Progress

Monitor performance, identify strengths and gaps, and work towards continuous self-improvement through structured progress insights.

04

Engage & Collaborate

Participate in discussions, connect with peers, and share ideas—fostering a collaborative academic environment even beyond classrooms.
