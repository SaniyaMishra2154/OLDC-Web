New Skill-Based Courses Launched at OLDC

Online Admission Portal Opens for 2025-26

Admissions Open for Certificate & Diploma Programmes (2025–26)

New Delhi | Published by OLDC, University of Delhi

Share This:
December 17

The Open Learning Development Centre (OLDC), University of Delhi, has opened admissions for its Certificate and Diploma Programmes for the academic session 2025–26, further strengthening its commitment to accessible, skill-oriented, and flexible higher education.

The newly announced admission cycle includes a range of programmes designed to support learners seeking academic upskilling, professional development, and practical competencies through open and distance learning modes.

Expanding Access to Skill-Based Education

The Certificate and Diploma Programmes offered by OLDC are structured to address the evolving academic and industry needs of learners from diverse backgrounds. These programmes aim to provide foundational knowledge, applied skills, and academic flexibility, particularly for students balancing education with work or other commitments.

By leveraging digital platforms and blended learning approaches, OLDC continues to extend educational opportunities beyond conventional classroom settings, ensuring wider reach and inclusivity.

Academic Focus and Learning Approach

The programmes emphasize:
  • Structured curriculum aligned with contemporary academic standards
  • Practical and skill-based learning components
  • Technology-enabled teaching and assessment models

Learners enrolled in these programmes will benefit from carefully designed course materials, academic guidance, and assessment frameworks developed in line with best practices in open learning.

Important Dates and Process

The admission process for Certificate and Diploma Programmes will be conducted in accordance with the academic calendar and guidelines issued by OLDC. Detailed information regarding eligibility, programme structure, and timelines is available on the official OLDC website.

Prospective learners are encouraged to review programme details carefully before applying.
