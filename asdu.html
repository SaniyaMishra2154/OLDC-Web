Advanced Software
Development Unit (ASDU)

The Advanced Software Development Unit (ASDU) is the in-house research and development unit of the Open Learning Development Centre (OLDC). This unit focuses on technology-driven solutions to enhance access, quality, and efficiency in open and distance learning, in alignment with the vision of NEP 2020.

ASDU was established to design and implement technology-driven systems that simplify academic processes, improve learner access, and create a seamless digital learning experience for open and distance learners.

01

Bridge gaps in access to quality education through technology

02

Improve the learning experience for Open and Distance Learners (ODLs)

03

Enable seamless, efficient, and scalable academic and administrative processes

04

Support institutional digital transformation initiatives

Alignment with NEP 2020

ASDU supports the objectives of the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020 by enabling technology-driven learning environments that improve learner access, support flexibility in learning, enhance academic delivery, and strengthen learner support systems.
