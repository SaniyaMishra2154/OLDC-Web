Career Services

At OLDC (Open Learning & Distance Campus), we understand that education is a stepping stone to a meaningful career. Our Career Services are designed to support students in transforming their academic achievements into professional success — even beyond the boundaries of a traditional campus.

We are committed to equipping our learners — wherever they are — with the tools, guidance, and opportunities needed to succeed in today’s competitive job market.
